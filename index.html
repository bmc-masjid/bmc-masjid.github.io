About the Center

Bangladesh Muslim Center Jame Masjid/Mosque is located in the vibrant Kensington area of Brooklyn. Established in 1994, it remains a pillar destination for the Bangladeshi Muslim community as well as other diverse Muslim communities fulfilling their daily spiritual and religious needs.

The mosque is dedicated to keeping the premises clean, well-maintained, and family-friendly. We hold daily five times prayers alongside regular Friday (Jummah) prayers.

Special Occasions & Services

We regularly host special community prayers including Eid Jamaat, Taraweeh prayers during Ramadan, and community events.
